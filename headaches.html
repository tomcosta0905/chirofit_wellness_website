Why Chiropractic Works for Headaches

If you're struggling with frequent headaches or migraines, chiropractic care may offer the relief you've been looking for. At Chirofit Wellness in Murrysville, PA, Dr. Ciara Shorts uses targeted spinal adjustments and cervical manipulation to address the root cause of tension headaches and cervicogenic headaches — the kind that originate in the neck and upper spine.

Many headaches are triggered by spinal misalignments that put pressure on surrounding nerves and muscles. Rather than masking the pain with medication, Dr. Shorts works to correct the underlying dysfunction so your body can heal naturally.

Patients across Pittsburgh, Monroeville, Murrysville, and Plum have found lasting relief through our nervous system-based approach. Whether you've been dealing with daily tension headaches or occasional migraines, we'll work with you to build a personalized care plan that targets your specific triggers.
