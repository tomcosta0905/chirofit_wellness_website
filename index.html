Meet Dr. Ciara Shorts

Dr. Shorts is a family chiropractor in Murrysville, PA dedicated to treating the root cause of discomfort through a nervous system-based approach. Her practice welcomes patients at every stage of life — from newborns to seniors.

With a commitment to long-term wellness rather than temporary relief, Dr. Shorts creates individualized care plans designed to help you move, feel, and live better — without relying on medication.
