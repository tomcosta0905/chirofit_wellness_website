Why Families Choose Chirofit Wellness for Pediatric Care

At Chirofit Wellness in Murrysville, PA, Dr. Ciara Shorts provides gentle, safe chiropractic care for infants and children. Pediatric chiropractic uses very light pressure — nothing like adult adjustments — to correct minor spinal misalignments that can interfere with your baby's nervous system development, sleep, and digestion.

Colic, fussiness, and feeding difficulties in infants are often linked to nervous system stress caused by the birthing process. Dr. Shorts' soft, nurturing approach helps restore balance to the nervous system, providing relief for both baby and parents.

Beyond colic, chiropractic care supports healthy development as children grow — from crawling and walking milestones to immune function and sleep quality. Families throughout Pittsburgh, Monroeville, Plum, and Murrysville trust Dr. Shorts for compassionate, effective pediatric chiropractic care.
